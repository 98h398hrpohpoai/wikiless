About

Wikiless is a free open source alternative Wikipedia front-end focused on privacy.

The source is available at Codeberg https://codeberg.org/orenom/wikiless.

  • All requests go through Wikiless, your browser never talks to Wikipedia
  • No JavaScript or ads
  • Self-hostable
